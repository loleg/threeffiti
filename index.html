This (α) project aims to quantify how graffiti imposes itself on the urban environment, exposing layers of subculture that are otherwise hidden. Using data analytics and computer graphics we juxtapose neighbourhood characteristics & the diverse styles of painters. Whether you consider them vandals, taggers or street artists, they are omnipresent in our cities, whose administrations & businesses wage endless, expensive battle to protect the walls from unwanted manifestations. This experiment could become a virtual gallery, a multifaceted data visualization, a shared online experiment. Works best with Google Chrome and a fast computer.
