This (ALPHA) project aims to ask what place graffiti & street art has in the urban environment, using 3D graphics to produce a juxtaposition of neighbourhood characteristics & tagging style. Inspired by artists who found their creative roots decorating inner city walls, provoked by web sites documenting the omnipresent and ephemeral works of elusive creators, this could be a virtual gallery adding the feeling of space & movement, a data visualization contrasting statistics of the inner city for new insights, or anything else the participants of a shared online experiment may imagine.
